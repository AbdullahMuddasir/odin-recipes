Burger

A simple homemade burger

Ingredients

  • cheese slice
  • Bun
  • Ketchup
  • Beef Patty
  • lettuce

Steps

  1. Put the beef Patty on a frying pan on low heat
  2. Toast the buns
  3. place the cheese slice on top of the beef patty
  4. Put ketchup and condiments of your choice on the buns
  5. Organise the burger and nom through it
